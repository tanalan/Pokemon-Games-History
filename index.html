Generation 1: The Kanto Region

The region based in the first Pokémon games, Red and Blue, the Kanto region is based on and named after the real Kantō region in Japan.

Theme

The cities and towns are named with the theme of colors

In 1999, Pokémon Gold and Silver were released, and starred the new Johto region. These games were revolutionary, bringing real colors and improved gameply into the games.

Generation 3: The Hoenn Region

Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire were soon out in 2002, and with it came the Hoenn region. Known for a "7.8/10 too much water" IGN meme, this region was the first to introduce a post-game battle area, the Battle Arena and the Battle Frontier (introduced in Pokémon Emerald).

The cities and towns naming seem to be a combination of two words that are interrelated
